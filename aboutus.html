About us

Our Destination Experts design custom-built itineraries through extensive research and rigorous negotiations with suppliers which reflect all the requirements of our clients. Our entire operation, from engaging with our clients to delivering the final product, is done online through calls, mails and texts. Our online presence can be discerned through our reviews across various social platforms. Headquartered at Boston, The Travel Fanatics caters to clients all over the states. We also offer our services to foreign nationals. In addition to providing holiday packages, we also extends live on-trip support to clients. Our Customer Dedication Personnel ensures the smooth execution of the entire tour, providing daily activity updates and instant grievance resolution to unforeseen circumstances. We know that empathetic client conversation, quick query handling, and delivering best priced services sets us apart from the rest of the industry. We pride ourselves on our genuine attention that we give to each of our client and we strive to work on it every day in order to make us better at what we do.
